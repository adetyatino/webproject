Plotting Stasiun GNSS

Plotting the movement of GNSS stations to determine the movement of the Semangko Timur-A fault and the Semangko Barat-B fault which is useful for predicting the maximum strength of the earthquake that occurs due to the two Semangko faults. This study is useful for PSHA (Probabilistic Seismic Hazard Analysis)
